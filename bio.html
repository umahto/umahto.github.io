who am I?

a lil about me

preferred pronouns: she/her/hers
dob: 11.29.1996
places i consider home: santa clara, california & bangalore, india & sunny san diego
hobbies: reading, writing, hiking, binge watching tv shows, napping

Hi! My name is Uma and I was born in November 1996 to a young, immigrant couple from India. I was born and raised in California up until 2008 when my family moved to India. I went to high school there but decided I wanted to pursue higher education in the States. I committed to UC San Diego in 2014 and my family moved back to California. I entered as a Bioengineering: Bioinformatics major, added a Cognitive Science minor at the end of my freshman year, changed that to a Design minor (that's a fun story for another time) at the end of my junior year, and will be graduating in Spring 2019!

I am passionate about technology, biology, design, research, STEM, education, and any combination of the previously listed. I care deeply about the environment along with diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) efforts and movements. I enjoy spending time outdoors, discovering new music (and then listening to the same song on repeat for hours), listening to my favorite podcasts and NPR, reading good books, and attempting to turn my thoughts in to poetry.
